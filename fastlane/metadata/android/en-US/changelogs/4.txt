Security hardening:
- TLS trust manager: checkClientTrusted now throws per contract; pins must be exactly 64 hex characters
- API-token login: explicit auth verification instead of inferring success from the node list
- New session-refresh helpers for 2h PVE tickets with a 10-minute skew
- Console WebSocket proxy: strict ticket/CSRF header validation and never logs ticket contents
- WebView JS console logs: DEBUG-only, truncated, and password/token strings are redacted
- Encrypted local blobs: AES-GCM with per-entry AAD binding and a silent v1 -> v2 migration
